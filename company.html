What drives us

Mission, values, principles
01 — Mission

Sovereign intelligence for everyone

Intelligence infrastructure that operates independently. No license servers, no telemetry endpoints, no activation requirements. Your hardware, your data, your rules.

02 — Values

Transparency over trust

Every binary compiled from audited source. Complete software bill of materials. Reproducible builds. Cryptographic attestation. Audit any layer, at any time.

03 — Principles

Holistic systems design

Hardware, software, and inference engine designed as one system. No integration tax. No impedance mismatch. Every component serves the mission.

Holistic systems have clear advantages in reliability, security, and sustainability. Challenging to implement but emphatically attainable.

Philosophy

Ergonomic to the mind.

An ergonomic chair doesn't ask you to adapt to it. It works with your spine's natural curvature, with gravity, with the way your weight distributes. You don't learn to sit in it — you just sit, and it's right.

We believe intelligence infrastructure should work the same way — shaped by the natural patterns of human cognition and decision-making. Surface the right information at the right moment. Respect attention as a finite resource. Protect the people who can't protect themselves from systems that extract value from their vulnerabilities.

The measure of good technology isn't what it can do. It's the space it creates for human beings to think clearly, act decisively, and be creative. Machines should handle the weight so people can do the work that only people can do.
