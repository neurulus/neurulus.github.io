The Neurulus power module is a non-lithium battery which is 1/4th of the cost of a lithium ion battery and less than half the cost of a lead acid battery in terms of gravimetric energy density.

Unlike the lithium ion battery, every single component of the Neurulus non-lithium battery is biodegradable and completely recyclable. They are manufactured using earth abundant natural resources and do not have supply constraints like lithium ion batteries.

But, most of all, the Neurulus non-lithium battery uses a safe electrolyte and hence there is no chance of an explosion and fire. So, they can be safely used to build megawatt scale grid storage batteries.
